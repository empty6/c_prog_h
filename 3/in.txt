Founded in 1881, Tokyo Tech celebrates its 135th anniversary
this year as Japan's top university in science and technology.
Throughout its long history, the Institute has trained outstanding
professionals for key roles in industry and academia. This is the
result of a distinguished education that equips students with
solid fundamental knowledge in science and technology, and
essential competencies in the social sciences and humanities.
Tokyo Tech provides an opportunity to experience the pleasure
of doing research at an early stage, encouraging students to
continue into further education, develop individual
specializations, and tackle cutting-edge research themes.
